## Featured projects

### 🗣️ Public Utility Crisis Sentiment Tracker
**Boca Chica & Santo Domingo Este, DR — June 2026**

End-to-end NLP pipeline that collects public comments from Instagram and Facebook, scores them with a Spanish transformer model (RoBERTuito), and serves a bilingual interactive dashboard — at zero hosting cost.

- **195 comments scored** across 9 public utility pages
- **71.3% negative** sentiment — EDEESTE (electricity) and CORAABO (water) drove most complaints
- Custom Dominican utility lexicon boosted 41% of scored comments
- Stack: `Python` · `pysentimiento` · `pandas` · `Apify SDK` · `Chart.js` · `GitHub Pages`

🔗 [Live dashboard](https://gabriel-matos13.github.io/sentiment-tracker/dashboard/) · [Repo](https://github.com/Gabriel-Matos13/sentiment-tracker)
